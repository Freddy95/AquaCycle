The Blacktip shark gets its name from the distinctive black markings on the tips of its fins. The shark lives off of most coastal waters near the equator and has a history of attacking humans 28 times unprovoked. The fish is target by several fisheries and its diet consists of many different kinds of fish including shrimp and porcupine fish.
