Controls

Keyboard Controls:

| W | Press this button to move forward

| A | Press this button to move to the left

| D | Press this button to move to the right

| SHIFT | Press this button to toggle moving slow and fast

| ESC | Press this button to enter and exit this menu


Mouse Controls:

While moving around, use your mouse to click on different objects in the game world. Finding new objects will earn you experience and facts about the objects will appear in the Objects Found tab of this menu.
